Product Details

This Pink Poly Cotton Anarkali Printed Kurta Palazzo Suit Set is a stunning ensemble, ideal for women seeking a blend of semi-formal elegance and festive charm. The set features an above-knee Anarkali kurta with a V-neck and 3/4th sleeves, beautifully complemented by palazzo pants. The kurta's soft pink color is adorned with an exquisite print, adding a touch of grace and femininity to the outfit. Made from a comfortable poly cotton blend, this suit set combines durability with a luxurious feel. Accessorize with pearl earrings and pink heels to enhance the ensemble's appeal, making it a perfect choice for weddings, festive celebrations, or any event where elegance is key.
