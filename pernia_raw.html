Shop Kalighata at Pernia’s Pop-Up Shop

This lineup of beautifully crafted Ethnic Wear comes from one of the most popular bridal shops in Kolkata. The brand has carved a niche for itself in the premium designer bridal wear segment. Brides from various parts of the country trust this brand for their stunning lehengas that would make them feel and look their best on their special day. From a small store in Salt Lake, Kolkata, this brand has now evolved into a sophisticated fashion range that comprises of Sarees, Lehengas, and a variety of other clothes for special occasions. The scintillating clothes from this brand are the result of the efforts of designers Ritesh Kamani and Neha Kamani. They focus on creating pieces that look and feel comfortable for a grand ceremony. Effortless elegance for Kalighata the brides is the brand’s forte. Whether you want Traditional Lehenga or dreamy gowns you will find an attractive variety in the Kalighata range at Pernia’s Pop-Up Shop.

 

Shop Kalighata Lehenga at Pernia’s Pop-Up Shop

To give the bride a memorable day, the brand offers some of the grandest Embroidered Lehenga. The intricately designed details on these lehengas will make heads turn. You will find the classic Blouse Tops and Dupatta combos crafted in chanderi or net fabric to make the bride star of the occasion. Embroidered patterns as well as those decorated with zardosi and sequins are all part of the collection. Besides these traditional styles, there are also the statement styles such as those that come with a jacket. Whether you are planning a summer or winter wedding, you will love the assortment of comfortable lehengas available in the Kalighata lehengas collection at Pernia’s Pop-Up Shop.

 

Shop Kalighata Gown at Pernia’s Pop-Up Shop

You might enjoy a little twist to your bridal outfit and this is when a Bridal Gowns might suit your needs. Full-sleeved gowns that will make you look regal have been designed with great care. The brand adds to the richness of the lehenga styles by utilizing colours such as silver and even subtle tones such as peach. These gowns have grand embellishments that add a sparkle to the bridal outfit.
